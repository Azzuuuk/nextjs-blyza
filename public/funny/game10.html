Categories Showdown

HOW TO PLAY:

Teams challenge each other to name items within a given category. A random category appears. Either team can "Call Bullshit!" on the other if they think that team can't list enough items within 30 seconds.

The challenged team then has 30 seconds to list as many valid items as they can. After the timer, if they succeeded, they get a point. If they failed, the challenging team gets the point. First to 5 points wins!
