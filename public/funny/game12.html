Word Wars

HOW TO PLAY:

  • Two team captains face the screen alone, where a secret word is displayed to only them
  • The captains engage in a bidding war, each claiming they can make their team guess the word using fewer descriptive words (e.g., "I can do it in 10 words," "I can do it in 8")
  • This continues until one captain believes the other cannot succeed with their low bid, at which point they challenge them to make their team guess the word
  • The challenged captain then uses exactly their declared number of descriptive words (that are different words and contain no part of the secret word)
  • If the team guesses correctly, the challenging captain's team earns a point. If they fail, the opposing captain's team gets the point!
