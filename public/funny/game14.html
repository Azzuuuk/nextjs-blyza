Connect The Dots

HOW TO PLAY:

  • Two letters appear (e.g., A & E).
  • Think of a word starting with the first letter and ending with the second.
  • The first team to shout a valid word gets a point!
  • First team to 5 points wins!
  • If stumped, press "Pass" for new letters.
