Connect The Dots

HOW TO PLAY:

  • Two letters will appear (e.g., A _ _ _ E).
  • Your team must think of a word that starts with the first letter and ends with the second.
  • The first team to shout out a VALID word that fits gets a point! The game master (or consensus) decides.
  • First team to 5 points wins!
  • If no one can think of a word, press "Pass" for new letters.
