Box of Lies

HOW TO PLAY:

  • A 2-player deception game. One player describes an image, the other guesses if they're telling the truth or lying.
  • The "Describer" clicks to reveal a random image (only they should see it).
  • The Describer can describe it truthfully or make something up!
  • The "Guesser" decides: TRUTH or LIE?
  • After the call, the Describer reveals if the guess was right.
  • Correct Guesser = point for their team. Wrong Guesser = point for the Describer's team.
  • First team to 3 points wins!
