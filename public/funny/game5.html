Box of Lies

HOW TO PLAY:

  • A 2-player deception game. One player describes an image they see, and the other must guess if they're telling the truth or lying.
  • The "Describer" clicks to reveal a random image (only they should see it initially).
  • The Describer then describes the image to the "Guesser" – they can be truthful or make something up!
  • The Guesser decides if the Describer is telling the TRUTH or LYING.
  • After the Guesser makes their call, the Describer reveals the image to everyone.
  • If the Guesser was CORRECT, their team gets a point. If WRONG, the Describer's team gets a point.
  • First team to 3 points wins!
